Logistics

What is my deadline to RSVP?

We've got plenty of time to the wedding...once invites are sent out our link to RSVP will go live and you will have until February of 2027.

Can I bring a date?

Please only bring a date if your invitation explicitly grants you a plus one!

Can I bring my kids?

Children listed on the invitation are welcome at both the ceremony and reception, but make sure they're not super annoying.

How can I get to the venue?

There will be some shuttle somethign from hotel. There are ubers. Our ceremony and reception are happening at the same place, so once you're there, you get to hang out with us all day. Check out the schedule page for more details.

What's the dress code?

Walter specifically requests Bend-core: flannels, patagonia vests, and berkenstocks only. Skylar specifically requests that you ignore him and wear something at least kind of nice. Just not white. Note for the ladies: ceremony will be on a grass lawn and dancing on a patio so we don't reccomend skinny heels.

Is the wedding indoors or outdoors?

Our ceremony will be outdoors, and the reception will take place both inside and out, weather permitting. Weather in Bend in May is general temperate, although it could get down into the 50s in the evening so bring layers.

Getting ahead of awkward conversations:

Is anyone changing their name?

Nope!

Are you doing wedding parties?

Nope! We've been together for many years and our friend group is completely integrated so we didn't want to make people pick sides. And we want all our friends to completely enjoy the wedding!

Can I give a toast?

So that we can have as much time to party and hang out with you as possible, the only toasts for the evening will be by our parents.

Can we give cash gifts?

Hell yeah (but say it more chill)

How late are you going to make us stay up?

Luckily for you we are not night owls and are planning on wrapping up our celebrations around 11pm. We would love if you could stay and enjoy the dancing, but also considering we have never stayed the whole time at weddings we've been to we certainly don't expect it from you.

If I have more questions, who should I contact?

Feel free to reach out to us at skylarandwalterwedding@gmail.com...although be warned our mothers might be the ones responding to your email so keep it classy.
